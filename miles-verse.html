Miles Morales returns for the next chapter of the Oscar®-winning Spider-Verse saga, Spider-Man™: Across the Spider-Verse. But when the heroes clash on how to handle a new threat, Miles finds himself pitted against the other Spiders and must set out on his own to save those he loves most. MOVIES

Miles Morales

Miles Morales is a fictional character in Marvel Comics. He first appeared in "Ultimate Fallout" #4 in 2011. Miles is a teenager who becomes the second Spider-Man in the "Ultimate" Marvel Universe. He has similar powers to the original Spider-Man, Peter Parker, but brings his own unique background and perspective to the role.

Spot (Dr. Johnathon Ohnn):

Spot, whose real name is Dr. Johnathon Ohnn, is a supervillain in Marvel Comics. He first appeared in "The Spectacular Spider-Man" #98 in 1984. His unique ability allows him to create portals in space, essentially making holes that can lead to other locations. He uses these abilities for criminal activities and has clashed with Spider-Man multiple times.

Miguel O'Hara (Spider-Man 2099):

Miguel O'Hara is a character from Marvel Comics and is known as Spider-Man 2099. He first appeared in "The Amazing Spider-Man" #365 in 1992. Miguel is a geneticist in a futuristic, cyberpunk-inspired world and gains powers similar to Spider-Man's through a genetic experiment gone awry. He becomes the Spider-Man of the year 2099, fighting crime in a high-tech, dystopian future.
